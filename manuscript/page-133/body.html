Mission Sideboard in Quarter-Sawed Oak

The rails are cut with tenon ends to match the mortises, and also have grooves to receive the panels. The bottom part of the back is closed with a panel and two rails, one at the same height from the floor as the front bottom rail, and the top one even with the under side of the top. The large panel is for the opening thus formed.

These parts are now put together, using plenty of good hot glue, and spreading it well on the mortises and tenon ends.
